I Don't Know What The Purpose of Life Is, But I Know What It Is At Least (poem)
Monday, November 30th, 2020

What is best in life?

That men should not die in the street.

What is the finest pleasure?

The finest, most discerning taste

Is for a lack of men dying in the street.

The most beautiful music

Is men not dying in the street.

The perfect work of sculpture

Is no men dying in the street.

The most divinely crafted story

Is no men dying in the street.
