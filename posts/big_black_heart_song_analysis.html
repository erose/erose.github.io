Big Black Heart by Better Oblivion Community Center
Saturday, April 27th, 2019

Today I'll be analyzing the song Big Black Heart by Better Oblivion Community Center, the collaboration between Phoebe Bridgers and Conor Oberst (of Bright Eyes).

This was a song that struck me from the blue. I probably had skimmed through it a few times without getting much. But one day something about it came together — I think it was at "Alright, alright for now" as the rhythm gets heavy — and I nearly cried.

There's a story that plays in my mind when I listen to Big Black Heart. (Though I don't think this is the only thing that could be going on.) You are in love with someone who is transitioning or has transitioned genders. Your love is of the type "hopeless crush"
