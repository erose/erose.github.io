About The Giveaway

Who's behind this?

This website was created by Open Philanthropy, a philanthropic foundation whose work is guided by the philosophy of effective altruism. Open Philanthropy is sponsoring the giveaway; students from EA Oxford are among those handling the logistics and book delivery.

Why is it free?

Some of Open Philanthropy's work is focused on addressing problems like the ones described in the book; "existential risks" to humanity that could end the whole human story. We've found the book to be helpful in our thinking about these problems, and the case for their moral urgency. We want to make the book widely available so everyone who's interested can engage with its ideas.
